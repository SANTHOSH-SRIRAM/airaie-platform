# Planning Archive

Superseded planning artifacts. Kept for traceability.

| Subdir | Contains |
|---|---|
| [`sprints/`](./sprints/) | Original PHASE_0..7 sprint breakdowns from 2026-04. Superseded by the [`../phases/`](../phases/) structure (numbered phase folders with per-step PLAN/RESEARCH/SUMMARY). |

## When to retire other planning artifacts here

When [`../phases/`](../phases/) entries are completed and milestones close, the per-phase folders get archived to a versioned subdir here (e.g., `archive/v1-phases/`). The `quick/` and `backlog/` folders never archive in bulk — entries are individually retired.
